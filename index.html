Tech Job Academy

An intensive, 16-week techincal training program preparing underemployed and unemployed NYC resident for in-demand tech jobs.

OVERVIEW

Tech Jobs Academy is a public-private partnership between Microsoft, CUNY's City Tech, and the Tech Talent Pipline. This technical training program targets passionate underemployed and unemployed NYC resident ready to launch a new tech career.

Student will spend 16 weeks developing highly sought-after skills in cloud and server administration, and gaining career development skills to land a job upon program completion.

APPLICATION PROCESS

  1. Check your eligibility

    Do you meet the following criteria?

  2. Fill out application form

    The application helps us get to know you better. Answer questions about your interest in technology and what gets you excited about the program.

  3. Take entrance assessment

    Learn the fundamentals of networking and take this online entrance assessment to prove your knowledge.

  4. Interview

    Selected candidates will be invited to interview for a spot in the cohort.

Eligibility Requirements

  • at least 18 years old
  • less than $50K salary
  • less than a 4-year degree
  • NYC resident
  • meet all federal selective service requirements
