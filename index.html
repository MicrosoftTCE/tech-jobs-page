Tech Job Academy

An intensive, 16-week techincal training program preparing underemployed and unemployed NYC resident for in-demand tech jobs.

OVERVIEW

Tech Jobs Academy is a public-private partnership between Microsoft, CUNY's City Tech, and the Tech Talent Pipline. This technical training program targets passionate underemployed and unemployed NYC resident ready to launch a new tech career.

Student will spend 16 weeks developing highly sought-after skills in cloud and server administration, and gaining career development skills to land a job upon program completion.
